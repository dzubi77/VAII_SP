About club

New York Rangers is hockey team playing in NHL with headquarters in Manhattan, New York. It plays in Eastern Conference's Metropolite division. Its home stadium is Madison Square Garden. Team's colors are: blue, white and red.

History

Club was created in 1926. By time they have became 4-times Stanley Cup winners, in years 1928, 1933, 1940 a 1994.
